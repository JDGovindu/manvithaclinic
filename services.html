Hair Loss and Alopecia Treatment

Hair loss, also known as alopecia, can be a distressing condition affecting both men and women. Our dermatologist specializes in providing expert advice and treatment recommendations for hair loss and alopecia.

Understanding Hair Loss:

Hair loss can result from various factors, including genetics, hormonal changes, medical conditions, and lifestyle factors. Alopecia, a specific type of hair loss, can lead to patchy or complete hair loss on the scalp or other parts of the body.

    Causes of Hair Loss:

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Hormonal changes, such as those related to pregnancy or menopause
  • Medical conditions, such as thyroid disorders or autoimmune diseases
  • Medications, such as chemotherapy drugs or certain blood pressure medications
  • Stress or traumatic events
  • Poor nutrition

    Treatment Recommendations:

  • Topical medications: A topical medication may be suggested to promote hair growth and slow down hair loss.
  • Oral medications: An oral medication may be recommended to reduce hair loss and promote hair regrowth in some cases.
  • Platelet-rich plasma (PRP) therapy: PRP therapy may be suggested to stimulate hair follicles and promote hair growth.
  • Low-level laser therapy: Low-level laser therapy may be recommended to stimulate hair follicles and promote hair growth.
